
For a loan taken for an amount $PV$ for $N$ periods at at an interest rate $r$ per period, the annuity $A$ is given as:

$$
\begin{align}
PV  &= \sum_{n=0}^N \frac{A}{(1+r)^n} = NA \sum_{n=0}^N \frac{1}{(1+r)^n} \\[12pt] A &= \frac{N \cdot PV}{1-(1+r)^{-N}} 
\end{align}
$$
